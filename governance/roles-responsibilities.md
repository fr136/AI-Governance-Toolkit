# Roles and Responsibilities

## Executive Management

- Approves AI strategy
- Accepts residual risks
- Allocates resources

## RSSI / CISO

- Defines security requirements
- Reviews AI-related risks
- Oversees incident management

## DPO

- Reviews privacy impacts
- Assesses GDPR compliance
- Advises on personal data processing

## Legal and Compliance

- Reviews regulatory obligations
- Validates contractual requirements

## IT Department

- Deploys approved AI solutions
- Manages access control
- Maintains technical security measures

## Business Owners

- Define business use cases
- Validate operational requirements
- Review generated outputs

## End Users

- Follow organizational policies
- Validate AI-generated content
- Report incidents and security concerns

## Internal Audit

- Reviews governance effectiveness
- Assesses compliance and control maturity
