# AI Steering Committee Charter

## Purpose

The AI Steering Committee provides oversight and governance for artificial intelligence initiatives.

## Objectives

- Review AI projects
- Validate risk assessments
- Monitor compliance obligations
- Review security concerns
- Track AI governance maturity

## Membership

- Executive Sponsor
- RSSI / CISO
- DPO
- Legal Representative
- IT Manager
- Business Representatives

## Responsibilities

### Governance

- Approve AI use cases
- Review governance documentation
- Monitor policy compliance

### Risk Management

- Review AI risk register
- Validate treatment plans
- Accept residual risks

### Compliance

- Monitor GDPR obligations
- Monitor AI Act requirements
- Review audit findings

## Meetings

Quarterly meetings or extraordinary sessions following significant incidents.

## Reporting

The committee reports governance status, risks and recommendations to executive management.
