# AI Incident Response Procedure

## Purpose

Provide a structured approach for handling incidents involving artificial intelligence systems.

## Examples of AI Incidents

- Confidential data exposure
- Personal data disclosure
- Unauthorized AI usage
- Hallucinated outputs causing business impact
- Prompt injection attempts
- Third-party AI provider compromise

## Response Lifecycle

### Identification

- Detect incident
- Record initial information
- Classify severity

### Containment

- Suspend affected services if necessary
- Restrict access
- Preserve evidence

### Investigation

- Determine root cause
- Identify impacted assets and data
- Assess regulatory implications

### Eradication

- Remove vulnerabilities
- Apply corrective actions
- Validate effectiveness

### Recovery

- Restore operations
- Verify controls
- Monitor for recurrence

### Lessons Learned

- Document findings
- Update policies
- Improve awareness and controls

## Roles

- Incident Manager
- RSSI / CISO
- IT Operations
- Legal and Compliance
- DPO
- Business Owner

## Reporting Requirements

Incidents must be documented and escalated according to organizational procedures.
