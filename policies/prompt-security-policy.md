# Prompt Security Policy

## Purpose

Protect organizational information when using generative AI systems.

## Scope

This policy applies to all users authorized to access AI services.

## Prohibited Information

Users shall not submit:

- Patient information
- Personal data
- Passwords
- API keys
- Internal reports
- Financial information
- Confidential contracts
- Security configurations

## Approved Uses

- Drafting content
- Summarization
- Translation
- Research support
- Training material preparation

## Security Controls

- Data classification before submission
- Human review of outputs
- Reporting of suspected incidents
- Compliance with internal policies

## Incident Reporting

Any suspected disclosure of confidential information through AI prompts must be reported immediately.

## Compliance

Failure to comply may result in disciplinary or corrective actions.
