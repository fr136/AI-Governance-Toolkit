# Human Validation Policy

## Purpose

Ensure that AI-generated outputs are reviewed and validated by qualified personnel before operational use.

## Principle

Artificial intelligence systems support human decision-making and shall not replace accountability.

## Validation Requirements

Human review is mandatory for:

- Security decisions
- HR decisions
- Legal documents
- Financial decisions
- Healthcare-related information
- Regulatory communications

## Reviewer Responsibilities

- Verify accuracy
- Verify relevance
- Verify compliance
- Identify hallucinations
- Validate final output

## Exceptions

Exceptions require documented management approval.

## Compliance

Failure to comply with this policy may result in disciplinary measures or corrective actions.
