# AI Acceptable Use Policy

## Purpose

This policy defines the authorized use of artificial intelligence technologies within the organization.

## Scope

This policy applies to employees, contractors, consultants, temporary workers and third-party providers.

## Authorized Use

Artificial intelligence tools may be used to:

- Draft documents
- Summarize information
- Support research activities
- Assist decision-making processes
- Improve operational efficiency

Human validation remains mandatory before any business decision.

## Prohibited Use

Users shall not:

- Upload confidential information to unauthorized AI services
- Process personal data without authorization
- Delegate critical decisions entirely to AI systems
- Circumvent security controls
- Generate misleading or fraudulent content

## Data Protection Requirements

Before using an AI solution, users must:

- Verify data classification
- Assess confidentiality requirements
- Confirm legal compliance
- Apply data minimization principles

## Roles and Responsibilities

### Users

Users are responsible for validating AI-generated outputs.

### Managers

Managers ensure compliance with organizational policies.

### Information Security Team

The security team defines controls, monitors risks and reviews AI-related incidents.

## Monitoring and Compliance

The organization reserves the right to audit AI usage activities to ensure compliance with internal policies and applicable regulations.

## Exceptions

Exceptions must be formally approved by management and information security representatives.

## Review Cycle

This policy shall be reviewed annually or following significant regulatory or technological changes.
